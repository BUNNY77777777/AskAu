Comprehensive Institutional Dossier:
Anurag University
Executive Summary and Institutional Genesis
Anurag University, situated in the rapidly developing educational corridor of Venkatapur,
Ghatkesar, Medchal–Malkajgiri District, Hyderabad, Telangana, stands as a premier
multidisciplinary private university
1
. The institutional lineage traces back to 1998 with the
establishment of its foundational undergraduate programs, which subsequently coalesced into
the Anurag Group of Institutions (AGI) in 2002
3
. Recognized as one of the first integrated
campuses in South India, the institution officially transitioned into a full-fledged university on
May 20, 2020
3
. This elevation was formally codified under the Telangana State Private
Universities (Establishment and Regulation) Act, 2018 (Telangana Act No. 11 of 2018), and its
subsequent 2020 Amendment Ordinance
2
.
Operated under the aegis of the Gayatri Educational and Cultural Trust (GECT), and
spearheaded by Chairman Dr. P. Rajeshwar Reddy alongside CEO Smt. S. Neelima, the
university has exponentially expanded its physical and academic footprint
3
. The campus
currently encompasses over 110 acres with a built-up infrastructure exceeding one million
square feet, designed to facilitate a dynamic, inquiry-focused learning environment
3
. The
university supports a robust demographic of over 15,000 active students and is backed by a
teaching and administrative fraternity of roughly 1,000 professionals, culminating in a global
alumni network exceeding 35,000 individuals
3
. By synthesizing interdisciplinary curricula,
rigorous outcome-based education, and profound industry integration, Anurag University has
positioned itself as a critical engine for technological and managerial talent in the region
3
.
Statutory Approvals, Accreditations, and National
Rankings
The institutional credibility of Anurag University is anchored by comprehensive endorsements
from India's highest statutory educational bodies, ensuring that all academic frameworks meet
rigorous national and international standards.
The university operates as a University Grants Commission (UGC) recognized autonomous
institution, explicitly included under Sections 2(f) and 12(B) of the UGC Act
3
. Quality assurance
is validated by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), which awarded the
institution an 'A' Grade, reflecting sustained quality parameters monitored since earlier cycles in
2007 and 2012, and peaking with an A++ assessment in specific sub-categories in 2021
5
. All
technical programs strictly adhere to the guidelines set by the All India Council for Technical
Education (AICTE)
3
. Furthermore, specialized disciplines operate under their respective
governing councils; the pharmacy programs are approved by the Pharmacy Council of India
(PCI), and the medical programs, facilitated through the affiliated Neelima Institute of Medical
Sciences, are sanctioned by the Medical Council of India (MCI) / National Medical Commission
(NMC) and the Indian Nursing Council (INC)
3
.
A defining hallmark of the university's engineering pedagogy is its Tier-I accreditation from the
National Board of Accreditation (NBA). This top-tier accreditation applies to flagship B.Tech
programs—including Civil Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Electrical &
Electronics Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Information Technology,
and Mechanical Engineering—and remains valid through June 30, 2025
6
. Additionally, the
university has secured corporate accreditation from Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), a
designation that significantly streamlines campus recruitment protocols
3
.
The university's trajectory in the Ministry of Education’s National Institutional Ranking
Framework (NIRF) illustrates a consistent presence among elite Indian institutions. The
institution maintained a position in the 101–150 Rank-band for Universities consecutively from
2021 to 2023, before stabilizing in the 151–200 band in subsequent general evaluations
3
. In
domain-specific NIRF rankings, the Engineering programs secured the 140th rank in 2022 and
146th in 2021, while the Pharmacy programs consistently rank within the top 100, reaching the
58th position in 2022, 61st in 2021, and 71st in 2024
5
.
Independent industry surveys corroborate this academic standing. The Times Top Private
Engineering Institute Rankings Survey positioned Anurag University as the 3rd Best Engineering
College in Telangana, the 10th Best in South India, and the 20th to 22nd Best nationally in
recent 2025 evaluations
5
. Historical metrics from Outlook and The Week consistently place the
engineering faculties within the top 60 nationally
6
. Furthermore, the university made its
inaugural appearance in the THE Sustainability Impact Rankings in 2026, signaling a growing
commitment to global environmental and developmental metrics
9
. Institutional accolades
include the Cisco Networking Academy Award for Best Performing Academy in Digital
Essentials, the Distinguished College Award from CSI TechNext India, and the Best Performing
College award conferred by the President of India through the Ministry of Youth Affairs and
Sports
5
.
Academic Architecture: Schools and Specialized
Curricula
Anurag University operationalizes its multidisciplinary vision through seven distinct academic
schools, collectively offering over 70 undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral degree
pathways. The academic structure is engineered to dismantle traditional silos, promoting
interdisciplinary research and industry-ready skill acquisition
9
.
The School of Engineering constitutes the largest and most dynamic academic faculty. It
provides comprehensive B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. programs across traditional and emerging
domains
5
. Traditional core branches include Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering,
Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE),
and Chemical Engineering
12
. To meet the demands of the modern digital economy, the school
heavily emphasizes advanced computing, offering specialized B.Tech programs in Computer
Science and Engineering (CSE), Information Technology (IT), Artificial Intelligence (AI), Artificial
Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML), CSE with Data Science, and CSE with Cyber Security
12
.
The curriculum is strictly aligned with industry expectations, focusing on algorithmic
foundations, secure system architectures, ethical hacking, and neural networks
18
.
The School of Management delivers a curriculum that bridges theoretical business
administration with practical, entrepreneurial execution. The flagship undergraduate offering is
the 4-year BBA (Honours) program, which allows students to specialize in high-growth sectors
such as Business Analytics, Fintech, Digital Marketing, Supply Chain Management, and
Operations Management
17
. At the postgraduate level, the Master of Business Administration
(MBA) is offered as a two-year, full-time program divided into six trimesters
10
. This structure
allows for an intense focus on Marketing, Finance, Human Resource Management, and
specialized pathways like Healthcare Management, which uniquely links students to a 300-bed
on-campus hospital for internships and dissertations
10
.
The School of Pharmacy, backed by PCI approvals, provides practice-driven pharmacological
education supported by clinical exposure and regulatory training. The academic portfolio
includes the 4-year Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm), the comprehensive 6-year Doctor of
Pharmacy (Pharm.D), a 3-year Pharm.D Post Baccalaureate, and specialized Master of
Pharmacy (M.Pharm) degrees focusing on Pharmaceutics, Pharmacology, Industrial Pharmacy,
and Pharma Analysis
5
.
Recognizing the global urgency for sustainable food production, the School of Agriculture has
pioneered a tech-enabled agrarian curriculum. The 4-year B.Sc. (Hons) in Agriculture strictly
follows the ICAR and NEP-2020 syllabus
10
. What distinguishes this program is the mandatory
integration of emerging technologies—such as Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things (IoT),
drone piloting, and Python programming—into traditional farming sciences
10
. Students utilize
over 50 acres of experimental farms and smart farming labs, culminating in a mandatory
20-week Rural Agricultural Work Experience (RAWE) that immerses them directly with local
farming communities
10
.
The School of Medical Sciences and the School of Nursing represent the university's foray into
critical healthcare education. These faculties are intrinsically linked to the Neelima Institute of
Medical Sciences and the fully operational Neelima Hospitals, providing students with
immediate, real-world clinical ecosystems
12
. The programs range from the foundational MBBS
degree to B.Sc. Nursing, alongside highly specialized Allied Health Sciences degrees such as
Medical Imaging Technology, Dialysis Technology, Anesthesia Technology, Emergency Medicine
and Critical Care, Respiratory Therapy, and Physiotherapy
12
.
Curriculum Framework, Pedagogy, and Academic
Regulations
Anurag University employs an Outcome-Based Education (OBE) framework, governed by
dynamic academic regulations that have evolved through the R20, R22, R23, and current R24
iterations
18
. This framework ensures that educational objectives directly translate into
measurable professional competencies and research capabilities.
The standard undergraduate engineering program spans four academic years, divided into
eight semesters, with each semester comprising exactly 16 weeks of rigorous instruction
19
. The
academic architecture dictates that students must complete their degree within a maximum
time frame of six years from admission. For students entering through the Lateral Entry
Scheme (LES) directly into the second year, the duration is compressed to three academic
years (six semesters), with a maximum completion window of five years
19
.
The academic journey begins with a foundational first year common to all engineering
disciplines, focusing heavily on Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and introductory
Programming in C
11
. Core, branch-specific subjects are systematically introduced beginning in
the second year
11
. Assessment strategies under the OBE model are bipartite, utilizing both
Continuous Internal Evaluation (CIE) and Semester End Examinations (SEE). For instance, a
standard 100-mark course allocates a significant portion of its weight to CIE, which evaluates
students through dynamic methods including case studies, assignments, quizzes, seminars,
group activities, and periodical tests, ensuring that theoretical knowledge is constantly tested
against practical scenarios
19
. Practical learning culminates in mandatory project work, including
a 5-week mini-project and a comprehensive 10 to 12-week major project evaluated internally
and externally
19
.
To foster interdisciplinary expertise without compromising foundational knowledge, the
university allows students to pursue specialized "Minors" or "Honours" tracks. While mid-course
branch changes are strictly prohibited to maintain cohort integrity, students maintaining a
CGPA of 7.5 by the end of their second semester with zero active backlogs can opt for an
18-credit Minor degree
10
. These highly sought-after minors cover frontier technologies
including Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cyber Security, Smart City Planning, Electric
Vehicles, VLSI, Robotics, and 3D Printing
10
. Furthermore, the university actively integrates
Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) and industry certifications into the grading system.
Faculty assist students in a blended learning mode to acquire certifications from global tech
giants such as Cisco, AWS, Oracle, IBM SkillsBuild, Microsoft Azure, and NPTEL, ensuring
graduates possess verifiable skills beyond the university curriculum10
.
A critical component of academic discipline is the stringent attendance policy. Following UGC
mandates, students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance per semester
10
. Falling short
of this threshold results in detention, prohibiting the student from being promoted to the
subsequent semester
10
. The university provides measured flexibility, allowing up to 25% of
working days to be excused strictly for verified medical leaves, and accommodating remedial
classes for students representing the university in recognized sporting events
10
.
Admissions Policy, Entrance Pathways, and Eligibility
The admissions process at Anurag University is engineered to be transparent, meritocratic, and
aligned with the directives of the Telangana Admission and Fee Regulatory Committee (TAFRC)
and state legislative frameworks
7
. The university operates a dual-pathway admission system to
ensure a diverse and highly capable student body.
The seat allocation matrix mandates that 70% of total seats are filled through the Telangana
State Engineering, Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test (TS EAMCET) counseling
process, strictly adhering to state government reservation policies, which explicitly includes a
25% reservation for students domiciled in Telangana
7
. The remaining 30% of seats constitute
the management and university quota, filled directly by the institution based on merit obtained
in national-level examinations or the university's proprietary entrance evaluation
7
.
The cornerstone of the university's independent admission and scholarship allocation is the
Anurag Common Entrance Test (AnuragCET). This examination mirrors the EAPCET syllabus
and is conducted in an offline format at the university campus. The assessment is a rapid,
90-minute test consisting of 80 multiple-choice questions—comprising 40 questions in
Mathematics, 20 in Physics, and 20 in Chemistry
3
. The examination design is student-friendly,
featuring no negative marking, with a baseline qualifying threshold set at 25% (20 marks)
3
.
Eligibility criteria are rigidly defined across all academic tiers.

Comprehensive Scholarship and Financial Aid Matrix
Anurag University operates a massive financial welfare initiative, mitigating economic barriers
to higher education. Institutional records indicate that over INR 18 Crores (and cumulatively
over INR 23 Crores since 2021) are disbursed annually through various scholarship vehicles
7
.
The primary vehicle for financial aid is the merit-based scholarship derived from the
AnuragCET examination. Outstanding performers who secure a rank between 1 and 10 are
awarded a 100% tuition fee waiver
7
. Candidates ranking between 11 and 25 receive a 50%
tuition waiver, and those placing between 26 and 100 earn a 25% fee concession
7
. To further
incentivize academic excellence, the university gifts highly configured laptops to the first 500
admitted students who qualify through AnuragCET and possess a 10+2 aggregate exceeding
75%
7
. Parallel merit scholarships are extended to students applying via national and state
exams; for instance, students holding TS EAMCET ranks up to 10,000 or JEE Main ranks up to
25,000 automatically qualify for a 50% tuition concession, with sliding scale waivers extending
to lower rank bands
7
.
The university is deeply committed to socio-economic inclusivity. Students belonging to
Scheduled Caste (SC) and Scheduled Tribe (ST) communities whose parental annual income
falls below INR 2 Lakhs are eligible for full scholarships or comprehensive tuition fee waivers
7
.
Similarly, students from Backward Classes (BC), Economically Backward Classes (EBC), and
Minority communities with a parental income under INR 1 Lakh receive substantial financial aid
7
.
The university also rewards athletic excellence, providing up to a 100% tuition waiver and
complimentary hostel accommodation to athletes demonstrating exceptional performance at
state or national levels
7
. Furthermore, a faculty welfare initiative grants up to a 75% tuition fee
waiver for the dependent children of university staff
7
.
Crucially, the retention of these financial privileges is contingent upon sustained academic and
behavioral excellence. Beneficiaries must maintain a minimum CGPA of 8.0, carry absolutely
zero active backlogs, strictly adhere to the 75% attendance mandate, and maintain an
unblemished disciplinary record free from any involvement in ragging or misconduct
7
. Failing
any subject instantly triggers the loss of fee concession eligibility for the subsequent academic
year
10
.

Campus Infrastructure and Academic Ecosystem
Anurag University is engineered to function as a self-sustaining microcosm, meticulously
designed to cater to the intellectual, physical, and psychological demands of its massive
student populace. The physical footprint of the 110+ acre campus is dominated by modern
academic blocks outfitted with smart, Wi-Fi-enabled classrooms, integrated audiovisual
projection systems, and vast open think-spaces
5
.
The research infrastructure is deeply specialized. The university houses over 20 dedicated
research centers and cutting-edge laboratories. These include advanced civil and mechanical
facilities like Concrete Technology and Heat Transfer labs, digital anatomy facilities for medical
students, and heavily equipped Internet of Things (IoT) centers for computer science scholars
5
.
The central library functions as the intellectual heart of the campus, holding a massive
repository of 78,200 physical volumes alongside active subscriptions to over 150 national and
international academic journals. This is supplemented by a comprehensive digital library
granting access to thousands of e-books and e-magazines, with extended operating hours
until 6:00 PM on all working days
26
. To aggressively foster an entrepreneurial culture, the
university operates dedicated innovation and incubation cells, most notably the HIVE
incubation center, which provides seed funding up to INR 2 Lakhs to nurture viable student-led
startup projects
5
.
Physical wellness and extracurricular development are facilitated by a massive 2-lakh square
feet (roughly 6,400 square meters) international standard sports complex

. The outdoor
facilities include a 150-meter wide cricket stadium, full-sized football fields, multiple basketball

. Indoor athletics are

table tennis, and chess

.
Medical security is absolute. The integration of the Neelima Institute of Medical Sciences and
Neelima Hospitals ensures that students have immediate access to a multi-specialty healthcare
facility. This on-campus hospital features an emergency ward with intensive monitoring beds,

.
Furthermore, the university mandates on-campus health insurance for its student body,

University operates a massive, precision-timed logistical network to ensure barrier-free
commuter access for its day scholars and faculty
54
.
The university maintains a formidable fleet of approximately 80 dedicated buses that penetrate
almost every residential sector of the twin cities of Hyderabad and Secunderabad
54
. The
operational mechanics are strictly synchronized with the academic timetable. Every bus is
scheduled to cross the university gates between 8:35 AM and 8:45 AM, ensuring all students
are present for the commencement of classes at 9:00 AM
54
. The return fleet departs the
campus collectively at exactly 4:20 PM, facilitating safe and early evening arrivals at home
54

Governance, Welfare, and Campus Discipline
To safeguard the immense student population and maintain an atmosphere dedicated to
academic rigor, Anurag University has instituted highly rigid governance protocols, overseen by
multiple specialized committees.
● Anti-Ragging Directives: The university maintains a zero-tolerance policy towards
ragging, defined as any physical or psychological abuse, bullying, or exclusion. This policy
is enforced by an active Anti-Ragging Committee comprising over 50 faculty members
10
.
As a primary preventative measure, all first-year students are isolated in a completely
separate academic block equipped with its own dedicated classrooms, laboratories,
canteen, and library, physically preventing unsanctioned interactions with senior cohorts
during their transition period
10
.
● Social Inclusivity and Grievance Redressal: The Prevention of Caste-based
Discrimination Committee (PCDC) and the Equal Opportunity Cell (EOC) actively monitor
the campus to ensure a bias-free environment for SC, ST, OBC, and socio-economically
disadvantaged groups (SEDGs). Students can instantly escalate grievances through
dedicated email channels or to the UGC-mandated Ombudsperson
50
.
● Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) & Women's Welfare: The ICC is strictly tasked
with preventing and addressing any incidents of sexual harassment, ensuring prompt,
confidential, and impartial investigations. This is supported by the Women's Welfare
Committee, which provides a safe platform for female students and staff to address
community concerns and welfare programs
50
.
● Health and Substance Policies: Anurag University enforces an absolute Smoke-Free,
Drug-Free, and Tobacco-Free Campus Policy. The possession or consumption of
cigarettes, vapes, e-cigarettes, gutka, khaini, alcohol, or any illicit drugs is banned across
all university-owned grounds, leased buildings, and vehicles. This is heavily monitored by
the Anti-Drug Committee, which collaborates with local police and legal experts to
conduct awareness sessions
50
.
● Dress Code: The university mandates a culture of professionalism. While there is no
specific uniform assigned (with the exception of the School of Management), a strict,
decent, and formal dress code is enforced for all male and female students within the
campus parameters
10
.

Concluding Synthesis
Anurag University represents a highly optimized, outcome-driven educational ecosystem.
Through its transition from a group of regional colleges to a massive, autonomous private
university, it has successfully established a curriculum architecture that mirrors the demands of
the modern global economy. By integrating emerging disciplines such as Artificial Intelligence,
Cyber Security, and precision Agriculture directly into its pedagogical core—and supporting
this with over INR 18 Crores in annual scholarships—the university ensures that merit
transcends socio-economic barriers.
For a prospective student integrating into this environment, the expectations are unequivocally
clear: intense academic rigor enforced through strict attendance and examination protocols, a
highly disciplined and structured residential life governed by unyielding curfew and safety
directives, and an unparalleled opportunity for corporate placement facilitated by a powerful
Corporate Resource Centre. Augmented by an exhaustive logistical transport network and a
vibrant, club-driven weekend culture, Anurag University systematically molds its student body
into highly capable, industry-ready professionals.
